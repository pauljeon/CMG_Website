About

Our Mission

Designing, Developing, and
Innovating for tech companies.

We bring fresh perspectives, unique ideas and detailed user research to the table. We want to allow students to collaborate and gain real world experiences through working with professionals.

Our Trade

The CMG approach: CMG understands that the client is always our first priority. Our team members are selectively handpicked and trained with the expertise surrounding your specific project. CMG dives directly into our client’s business challenges and believes in making a true difference in all our projects.

CMG undertakes 3 projects each semester and each project consists of two project managers along with four to five analysts, one senior analyst and two project advisors
